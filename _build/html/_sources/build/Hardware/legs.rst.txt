Assembling the Foot
-------------------

Attach the metal foot to the metal servo mount

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_203400.jpg
    :height: 250px

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_203311.jpg
    :height: 250px

Attach the 3D printed ankle to a metal U connector

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_212323.jpg
    :height: 250px

Place the bearing into the metal U connector, place the 3D printed washer over the top and secure this to the foot

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_210032.jpg
    :height: 250px

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_210058.jpg
    :height: 250px

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_210242.jpg
    :height: 250px

Attach a motor grip to a motor

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_210510.jpg
    :height: 250px

Place the motor in the motor bracket and secure it to the bracket and U connector

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_211338.jpg
    :height: 250px

.. image:: Photos/Leg/20180602_211743.jpg
    :height: 250px

Foot assembly is now complete!
